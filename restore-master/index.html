About

Restore is a top down 3D action adventure game, set in a dying world infected by a crystal virus.
With our unique Live Action Point (LAP) system you’ll have to be resourceful with your attacks in order to survive.
The game was made as a school project at the end of 2019 by us 4 students with a timeframe of 10 weeks.
As of right now the school project version of the game is now available for download.

There has been no further plans with production yet.
